GET TOGETHER: PRINTMAKING

June 26, 2014 Margot van der Krogt

In our continuing collaboration with SIMPLY SLOW , a growing community embracing the art of authentic crafts, we shared our interview with illustrator and designer Maartje van den Noort for June's focus, PRINTMAKING. You can also read the review of the Sunday afternoon get together on SIMPLY SLOW's Blog .

The summer solstice has passed, the sun is shining and the air is warm.
As we pass Park Frankendael in the East of Amsterdam, we hear the birds chirping and take in the sweet smell of the wildflowers in the fields. We finally arrive at the location of the Grafisch Werkcentrum Amsterdam.
Hidden in a row of warehouses, it’s like a secret waiting to be told.
Eager to dive into the traditional craft of printmaking, we step inside.

Collaboration with SIMPLY SLOW

January 23, 2014 Margot van der Krogt

We're excited to announce our collaboration with SIMPLY SLOW , a growing community embracing the art of authentic crafts. Every month, they organize small get togethers around one craft, like the making of beer, bread and coffee, and the craft ing of wool, jewel and ceramics.

Last weekend we joined together for an afternoon centered around JEWEL with Maker Marike Hauser of atelier LUZ . A report of the event can be found on the SIMPLY SLOW Blog ...

We met on a rainy Sunday afternoon in January.
NLstudio’s, a hidden gem in the Jordaan, was a beautiful location for our celebration
of the fine craft of jewelry making with Marike Hauser of atelier LUZ.
The simple yet serene interior exuded enormous warmth, welcoming us inside.

CreativeMornings Amsterdam

December 18, 2013 Margot van der Krogt
