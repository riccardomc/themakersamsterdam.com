The Makers Amsterdam was started in 2013 by Margot van der Krogt as an attempt to uncover and portray Amsterdam's creative entrepreneurs, or 'makers'.

Follow the project on Twitter ( @themakersams ) and Facebook ( themakersamsterdam ). Check out Instagram ( #themakersamsterdam ) for behind-the-scenes photos. Would you like to get involved, partner with us or suggest a Maker? Send an email to themakersamsterdam@gmail.com .

Founder & Editor
Margot van der Krogt is a maker enthusiast based in Amsterdam, the Netherlands. She works as a freelance copywriter, editor and content specialist at Mae & Many , and hosts the Amsterdam chapter of CreativeMornings . Follow Margot on Instagram , Facebook and Twitter .

Contributor
Hannah Fuellenkemper is an eater and an editor, a freelance writer and a cook. She works as a copywriter and food stylist when working, and is developing her own written portfolio when not. Follow Hannah's blog or Instagram .
